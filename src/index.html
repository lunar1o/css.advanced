About

There are many music apps available, but IO is the first app created with Artificial Intelligence.

With IO, you can listen to music tailored specifically to your tastes.

The app learns what you like and makes recommendations and even creates perfect music based on that information. It can create custom playlists for you based on your activity or mood. Whether you're working out, relaxing, or studying, IO has a playlist for you. And also offers a social component, allowing you to share your favorite music with friends and see what they're listening to as well.

It is a highly user experience focused application, always focusing on improving the experience for the listeners. With a clean and user-friendly interface that makes it easy to find and listen to your favorite songs, IO identifies which sounds you may feel comfortable with or not. Only then, offer recommendations and playlists with AI created music.
